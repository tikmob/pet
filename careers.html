Certified Dog Trainer

Remote / On-site Full-time $45,000 - $65,000

We're seeking an experienced Certified Dog Trainer to develop training content, conduct virtual training sessions, and contribute to our educational resources.

Requirements:

  • Certification in dog training (CPDT-KA preferred)
  • 3+ years of professional dog training experience
  • Excellent communication and teaching skills
  • Experience with positive reinforcement methods
  • Ability to create engaging training content

Pet Care Content Writer

Remote Full-time $40,000 - $55,000

Join our content team as a Pet Care Writer, creating informative and engaging articles about dog training, cat behavior, nutrition, and general pet care.

Requirements:

  • 2+ years of professional writing experience
  • Strong knowledge of pet care and animal behavior
  • SEO writing experience preferred
  • Ability to research and verify scientific information
  • Passion for animal welfare and education

Veterinary Consultant

Remote Part-time Contract

We're looking for a licensed veterinarian to consult on content accuracy, review articles, and provide expert insights for our pet care resources.

Requirements:

  • DVM degree and active license
  • 5+ years of clinical experience
  • Special interest in behavior or nutrition
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to explain complex topics simply
